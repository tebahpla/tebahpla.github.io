Add Oogle to Chrome

To add Oogle to chrome, right-click the  Omnibox and click ¨Edit Search Engines¨

A window like this one will appear.  Scroll down to Other Search Engines and click “ADD”.

 A pop-up will pop up so in the first box, type, Oogle .

In the second box type, oogle.gq.  In the third, type,    https://tebahpla.github.io/newoogle?q=%s, click ADD. After that, click the three dots then “Make default”  and Oogle is your default search engine!

Add Oogle to Firefox


  1. Install this extensoin
  2. Right-click the search bar below and choose "Add To Search Bar..."
  3. A pop-up will appear. There will be two input boxes. In the first one type, "Oogle" In the second type, "oogle.gq"
  4. Copy about:preferences#search and paste it into a new tab
  5. Use the drop-down menu to change your defaault search engine. Oogle will be at the bottom.
