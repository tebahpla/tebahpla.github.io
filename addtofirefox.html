Add Oogle to Firefox


    On Firefox 57 or newer:

  1. Click the
  2. Click "Add Search Engine"
    3. Now Oogle is on your search engine list. To make it the default go to about:preferences#Search and choose Oogle from the dropdown menu.

    On Firefox 56 or older:

  3. Install this extensoin
  4. Right-click the search bar below and choose "Add To Search Bar..."
  5. A pop-up will appear. There will be two input boxes. In the first one type, "Oogle" In the second type, "oogle.gq"
  6. Copy about:preferences#search and paste it into a new tab
  7. Use the drop-down menu to change your defaault search engine. Oogle will be at the bottom.
